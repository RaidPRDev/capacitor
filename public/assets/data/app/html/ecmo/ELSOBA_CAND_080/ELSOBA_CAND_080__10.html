VV ECMO:
This is recommended as a first choice mode in all neonates (regardless of vasoactive infusions)
  • Patient weight > 2.5 kg
VA ECMO:
This is recommended for severe biventricular cardiac dysfunction
  • Significant pneumopericardium
  • Weight < 2.5 kg
  • CPR
This completes the assessment of neonates with Acute Respiratory or Cardiac Failure, non-CDH or Cyanotic CHD for ECMO.
