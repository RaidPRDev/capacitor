  • Surfactant
  • High PEEP strategy
  • HFOV with increased MAP to recruit
  • Chest tube(s)
For PPHN:
  • Low PEEP strategy
  • Inhaled pulmonary vasodilators
  • Prostaglandin E1
For Air Leak Syndromes:
  • Chest tube(s)
  • Low PEEP vs HFOV vs HFJV
