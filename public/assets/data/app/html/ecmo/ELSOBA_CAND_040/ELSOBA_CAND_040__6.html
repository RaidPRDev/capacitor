Check contraindications for ECPR. Contraindications include:
  • Age > 70
  • Arrest to first CPR > 5 minutes
  • Asystole as the initial rhythm
  • ETCO2 < 10 mm Hg during CPR
  • Arrest to ECMO flow > 60 minutes
  • Known severe aortic insufficiency
  • Not suitable for ECPR from premorbid condition (e.g. end-stage heart or lung disease, ESRD, liver failure with cirrhosis, severe neurologic impairment, terminal illness) or advanced directives
