Consider:
  • Deepened sedation or neuromuscular blockade
  • Diuresis
  • Improve pulmonary blood flow
  • Optimize PEEP
  • Prone positioning
  • iNO
  • Drainage of pleural fluid
  • Transfusion for optimized oxygen content
  • Non-conventional ventilation
  • Assess oxygenation adequacy with reduced supports (supplemental and/or vent weaning).
This completes the assessment of children with acute hypoxemic respiratory failure for ECMO.
