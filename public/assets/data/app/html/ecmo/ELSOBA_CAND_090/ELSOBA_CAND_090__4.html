Relative contraindications include:
  • Severe congenital anomalies (i.e., major cardiac anomalies)
  • Lethal chromosomal abnormalities or other lethal malformations
  • Grade III or IV intracranial hemorrhage
  • Weight <1.5 - 2 kg
  • Gestational age <32-34 weeks
  • Prolonged mechanical high pressure ventilation
  • Uncontrolled hemorrhage
