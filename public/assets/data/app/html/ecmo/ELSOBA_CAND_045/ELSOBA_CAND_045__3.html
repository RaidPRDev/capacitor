Does the patient demonstrate one or more indications despite optimal management?
Indications include:
  • PaO2/FiO2 <80 mmHg >6 hours OR <50 mm Hg >3 hours
  • Ppl >30 cm H2O on lung protective ventilation
  • pH <7.25 with PaCO2 >60 mmHg >3 hours
Optimal management strategies include:
  • Strongly Recommended:

    • Prone positioning (unless contraindicated)
  • Recommended:

    • Neuromuscular blockade
    • High PEEP strategy
  • Consider:

    • Inhaled pulmonary vasodilators
    • Recruitment manoeuvres
