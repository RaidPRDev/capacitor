Consider Patient for ECMO Candidacy

The patient presents with signs and symptoms and/or a diagnosis where a mode of ECMO should be considered.
Can your institution manage the entire care continuum for this patient?
Care continuum involves considerations of:
  • Mode of ECLS (VA, VV, etc.) or additional support devices
  • Support type (Cardiac, pulmonary, etc.)
  • Availability of transplant services
  • Access to transport services (internal or from referral institutions)
  • Established protocols with referral institutions regarding cannulation and transport
