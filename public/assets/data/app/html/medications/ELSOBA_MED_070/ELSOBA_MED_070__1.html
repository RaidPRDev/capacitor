Prothrombin Complex Concentrate:
Adult dose:
Normalize Prothrombin time (PT) or INR
  • Desired increase in PT (%)*kg bodyweight = IU of 4Factor PCCs
  • INR 2-4: 25 IU/kg of 4Factor PCCs /kg bodyweight
  • INR 4 to 6: 35 IU of 4FactorPCCs/kg bodyweight
  • INR greater than 6: 50 IU of 4FactorPCCs/kg bodyweight
Treatment of bleeding with prolonged PT/INR or CT on ROTEM
  • 20-30 IU/kg 4Factor PCCs ( ~1500-2000 unit)
ECMO:
After administration watch out for signs of clotting!
