Cryoprecipitate (Contains fibrinogen, vWF, FXIII):
Target:
  • Active, clinically relevant bleeding or planned surgical intervention: Fibrinogen >150mg/dl;
  • No bleeding: Fibrinogen >100mg/dl
Adult dose:
1 U/5-10 kg bodyweight (~6-10 Units)
Pediatric dose:
Cryoprecipitate 1 unit/5 kg
ECMO:
After administration watch out for signs of clotting!
