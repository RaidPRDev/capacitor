Fibrinogen:
Target:
  • Active, clinically relevant bleeding: Fibrinogen > 150 mg/dl
  • No bleeding: Fibrinogen > 100 mg/dl
Adult Dose:
For fibrinogen levels < 1.5 g/L with active bleeding.
  • Standard Dosing: ~ 50 mg/kg (2- 4 g) adjusted based on fibrinogen levels
  • Calculated Dosing: desired increase (g/L) x 0.04*kg of bodyweight
Pediatric Dose:
50 mg/kg
ECMO:
After administration watch out for signs of clotting!
