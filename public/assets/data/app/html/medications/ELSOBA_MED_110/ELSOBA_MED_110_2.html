Fentanyl: Intravenous opioid
ECMO dose adjustment: YES—an increased dose may be warranted in some patients.
Renal impairment dose adjustment: NONE
CRRT dose adjustment: NONE
Hepatic impairment dose adjustment*: YES—a decreased dose may be warranted in some patients.
Adult starting dose^
Intermittent: 0.35-0.5 mcg/kg IV every 30-60 min (25-50 mcg IV every 30-60 minutes)
Continuous infusion: 0.7-10 mcg/kg/hr (25-500 mcg/hr)
Pediatric starting dose^
Intermittent: 1-1.5 mcg/kg IV every 30-60 min
Continuous infusion: 1-1.5 mcg/kg/hr
Neonatal starting dose^
Intermittent: 1-1.5 mcg/kg IV every 30-60 min
Continuous infusion: 1-1.5 mcg/kg/hr
*Dose adjustment will be dependent on acuity and severity of impairment.
^Always use the lowest-effect dose to an objective endpoint (e.g., pain).
