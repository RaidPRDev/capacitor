Morphine:
Intravenous opioid
ECMO dose adjustment:
YES, increased dose may be warranted in some patients
Renal impairment dose adjustment*:
YES—decreased dose may be warranted in some patients
CRRT dose adjustment:
YES—a decreased dose may be warranted in some patients.
Hepatic impairment dose adjustment*:
YES, decreased dose may be warranted in some patients
Adult starting dose^
Intermittent: 0.01-0.15 mg/kg IV every 1-2 hrs (2-4 mg IV every 1-2 hrs)
Continuous infusion: 0.07-0.1 mg/kg/hr (1-4 mg/hr)
Pediatric starting dose^
Intermittent: 0.05-0.1 mg/kg IV every 30-60 min
Continuous infusion: 0.05-0.1 mg/kg/hr
Neonatal starting dose^
Intermittent: 0.05-0.1 mg/kg IV every 30-60 min
Continuous infusion: 0.05-0.1 mg/kg/hr
*Dose adjustment will be dependent on acuity and severity of impairment.
^Always use the lowest-effect dose to an objective endpoint (e.g., pain).
