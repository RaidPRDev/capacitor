Refer to DTT/ECA target only.

Search for other causes of prolonged aPTT:
  • F VII, F IX, fibrinogen deficiency (>> consider repletion)
  • F XII deficiency (common, no increased bleeding risk)
  • Lupus anticoagulant, antiphospholipid antibodies (increased risk for thrombosis).

No change in DTI dose.

Continue dTT/ECT guided anticoagulation.

Continue dTT/ECT guided anticoagulation.

Consider repeating labs.

Consider hypercoagulable state.
