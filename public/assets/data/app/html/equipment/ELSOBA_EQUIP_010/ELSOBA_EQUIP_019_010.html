The choice of the cannula is guided by:
  • The aimed degree of support
  • The size and condition of the vessel
  • Patient size
  • Possible site of placement
  • Type of insertion procedure
  • Desired location of blood drainage/return
Cannula size
  • The outer diameter of the cannula
  • Expressed in French (1FR = 0.33mm)
  • In accordance with the desired amount of flow
  • Can be guided using the pressure drop/flow charts
